### 2. Frequently Asked Questions (+990 words)
**Location**: Between Management and Contact sections
**ID**: `#faq`

**9 Questions Covered**:

1. **What types of properties does Berez Investment Group acquire?**
   - Property types, size, class, unit mix details

2. **Why do you focus on B and C class apartments instead of luxury properties?**
   - Workforce housing advantages, cash flow, competition, value-add potential

3. **What geographic areas do you invest in?**
   - Primary markets (East Portland, Gresham, Troutdale, Fairview)
   - Secondary markets (Clackamas, Washington County)
   - Why these markets work

4. **How do you finance property acquisitions?**
   - Conventional financing approach
   - Assumable loans strategy
   - Conservative leverage targets

5. **What returns do you target on acquisitions?**
   - Cap rates, cash-on-cash returns
   - Hold period expectations
   - Conservative underwriting approach

6. **Do you accept outside investors or operate as a fund?**
   - Current structure (private investment company)
   - Not a fund, no outside investor solicitation
   - Open to case-by-case partnerships

7. **What is your typical renovation and repositioning timeline?**
   - Light renovations: 12-24 months (as units turn)
   - Major improvements: 6-12 months front-loaded
   - Approach to minimize disruption

8. **How do you handle property management?**
   - Third-party management + active oversight
   - Monthly reviews, quarterly inspections
   - Owner approval authority on major decisions

9. **Are you actively looking to buy more properties right now?**
   - Yes, actively seeking opportunities
   - Current market dynamics favoring acquisitions
   - Call to action for property submissions

**SEO Value**:
- Targets long-tail keywords naturally
- "People Also Ask" optimization
- Rich snippet eligibility (FAQ schema potential)
- Answers common investor/broker questions
- Keywords: multifamily investment, B class apartments, Portland real estate, cap rates, property management, value-add, financing
